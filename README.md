This repository contains an end-to-end Sales Analytics Dashboard developed in Power BI using structured business data. The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of sales performance, revenue trends, and profitability metrics, enabling stakeholders to make data-driven strategic decisions. 

The project demonstrates strong capabilities in data modeling, business analysis, and visual storytelling.

## Project Overview

The goal of this project is to analyze Elite Group's 2021 sales data and answer practical business questions such as:

- Which products and categories generate the highest **Total selling value** and **Profit**?
- How do sales and profit behave by **Month** and **Day**?
- Which **Sale Types** (Direct, Online, Wholesaler) perform better?
- How do different **Payment Modes** (Cash vs Online) contribute to sales?

## Business Problem

Organizations often struggle to:

Track overall sales and revenue performance

Identify top-performing products, regions, and customers

Understand profitability trends over time

Make quick, insight-driven decisions from raw data

This dashboard addresses these challenges through a single, interactive analytical view.

## Key Insights Delivered

Total Sales & Revenue KPIs for high-level performance monitoring

Trend analysis to identify growth patterns and seasonality

Product and category-level performance evaluation

Region / segment-wise contribution analysis

Clear visibility into profitability drivers and gaps
